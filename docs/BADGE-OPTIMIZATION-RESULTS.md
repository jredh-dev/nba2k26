## Key Insights

### Height Impact
- **Shorter centers (6'9" - 6'10"):** Superior finishing attributes and mobility (Speed, Agility)
- **Taller centers (7'0"+):** Marginal defensive improvements, significant finishing penalties
- **Sweet spot:** 6'10" balances finishing, defense, and badge availability

### Wingspan Impact
- **Moderate wingspan (+0 to +4 inches):** Optimal badge availability
- **Long wingspan (+6+ inches):** Diminishing returns, may reduce some attributes
- **Sweet spot:** +2 to +4 inches over height

### Weight Impact
- **Light weight (220 lbs):** Better Speed and Agility
- **Heavy weight (260-280 lbs):** Better Strength, slightly worse mobility
- **Badge availability:** Minimal difference (32-34 badges across 220-280 lbs range)
- **Sweet spot:** 220-240 lbs for mobility-focused, 260+ lbs for physical play

### Badge Patterns
1. **Finishing dominates:** 19/34 badges (56%) are Finishing category
2. **Defense accessible:** 6 defense badges available even for offense-focused builds
3. **Playmaking strong:** 5 high-tier playmaking badges (3 Legendary)
4. **Rebounding limited:** Only 2 rebounding badges
5. **Secondary badges:** Many badges have ANY-attribute requirements, making them easier to unlock
